### **Step 1: Select Logo**
1. Click **"Select Logo"** button
2. Choose an image file (PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF)
3. File must be under 5MB
4. Preview will appear instantly

### **Step 2: Upload Logo**
1. Click **"Upload Logo"** button
2. Wait for upload to complete
3. Success message will appear
4. Logo is now saved

### **Step 3: Save Lab Information**
1. Fill in laboratory details (name, address, phone, etc.)
2. Click **"Save Laboratory Information"**
3. All settings are now saved

### **Step 4: Verify Logo Appears**
1. Go to **Billing** and create an invoice
2. Go to **Reports > View Reports**
3. Logo should appear in document headers
